The Problem

Students at my school want snacks, but the vending machines only have junk food. Parents complain about unhealthy options, kids are hungry during after-school activities, and the school doesn't have budget for healthy alternatives. Meanwhile, there's growing demand for treats that don't compromise on nutrition.

The Solution

Bright Bites Bakery makes cookies using natural sweeteners and whole ingredients that actually taste good. We sell them at school events, through weekly pre-orders, and at the local farmers market. Each cookie comes with nutrition info so parents know exactly what their kids are eating.

Our secret? We replaced refined sugar with honey and dates, use whole wheat flour, and add protein through nut butters. The result is a cookie that's 40% less sugar than store-bought options but still feels like a real treat.

Target Market

Primary: Health-conscious parents of elementary and middle school students in the Sacramento area who want better snack options for their kids.

Secondary: High school athletes looking for post-workout snacks and local businesses seeking healthier catering options for meetings.
