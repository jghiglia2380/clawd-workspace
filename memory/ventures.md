## PFL Academy

**Target:** High schools in states with financial literacy graduation mandates
**Pricing:** $26/student
**Focus:** Full semester curriculum (daytime instruction, not enrichment)
**Rigor:** High — designed to meet state mandate requirements

### Structure
- 69 chapters
- Day 1/Day 2 format per chapter
- Integrated simulators and skill builders
- Teacher guides
- AI support features

### Freemium Model

**Free Tier (Lead Gen):**
- PDF downloadable resources
- 12 universal categories:
  1. Careers & Income
  2. Education
  3. Budgeting
  4. Taxes
  5. Banking
  6. Investing
  7. Retirement
  8. Credit & Loans
  9. Housing
  10. Insurance
  11. Consumer Protection
  12. Economics & Markets
- 552 state-specific PDF resources

**Paid Tier ($26/student):**
- Interactive simulators
- AI tools and support
- LMS integration (Canvas, Schoology)
- Automated grade passback
- State-specific personalization (86+ variables)
- 62 state curriculum configurations
- Localized content (min wage, housing costs, etc.)

### Conversion Funnel
Free PDFs → Teachers experience quality → Convert to paid platform
