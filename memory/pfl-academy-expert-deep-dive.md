## What PFL Academy IS

**A full-semester high school financial literacy curriculum for graduation credit and letter grades.**

NOT enrichment. NOT business plans. NOT entrepreneurship (that's Startup Smartup).

---

## Curriculum Structure

### 15 Standards, 40+ Chapters

| Standard | Topic | Chapters |
|----------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Careers (Jobs vs. Careers, Income, Taxes, Financial Goals) | 5 |
| 2 | Taxes (Federal/State, Brackets, Filing, Deductions, Planning) | 5 |
| 3 | Financial Service Providers | 2 |
| 4 | Managing a Bank Account | 1 |
| 5 | Saving and Investing (Compound Interest, Strategies, Risk) | 5 |
| 6 | Retirement Planning | 2 |
| 7 | Borrowing Money (Credit, Credit Score, Student Loans) | 5 |
| 8 | Credit Cards and Online Shopping | 2 |
| 9 | Fraud and Identity Theft Protection | 2 |
| 10 | Renting vs. Buying (Housing Decisions, Leases, Home Buying) | 3 |
| 11 | Risk Management and Insurance | 3 |
| 12 | Gambling Risks | 2 |
| 13 | Bankruptcy and Debt Management | 1 |
| 14 | Charitable Contributions | 2 |
| **15** | **Career Readiness (Resume, Interview, Workplace Skills)** | **5** |

---

## Day 1 / Day 2 Structure

Each chapter has **two days**:

### Day 1: Content Guide
- Direct instruction / reading
- Key concepts and vocabulary
- Learning objectives
- Initial activities

### Day 2: Learning Lab
- **Hands-on application activities**
- Learning Stations (typically 3-4 per chapter)
- Interactive scenarios and case studies
- Self-assessments
- Portfolio-building activities
- AI-powered feedback and simulations

## AI-Powered Features

### Currently Implemented
1. **Reflection Analysis** - OpenAI-powered feedback on student written responses
2. **Interview Simulator** - Select career field, position type, interview format → AI generates tailored questions
3. **AI Feedback on Portfolio Work** - Evaluates completeness and depth of responses

- **Frontend**: React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
