## Loop Marketing Strategy

### The Four Loops

1. **Teacher-to-District Loop**
   - Teachers use platform ‚Üí Experience success ‚Üí Advocate internally ‚Üí District adopts ‚Üí More teachers

2. **Student Artifact Loop**
   - Students use skill builders ‚Üí Create impressive artifacts ‚Üí Share with parents/counselors ‚Üí Inquiries to school ‚Üí New districts

3. **Implementation Data Loop**
   - District implements ‚Üí Generate success data ‚Üí District shares publicly ‚Üí Other districts see evidence

4. **Content Loop**
   - Write Substack ‚Üí Policy insights resonate ‚Üí Readers reach out ‚Üí Conversations ‚Üí Relationships

### Primary Metric
**WAR (Weekly Active Relationships)** = Districts/schools with meaningful engagement this week
- Target: 3% weekly growth
- Compounding: 3% weekly = 4x annual

### Operating Cadence
- Monday Morning Reset (30 min)
- Wednesday Midweek Velocity Check (15 min)
- Friday Loop Audit (20 min)
