## NEBRASKA ✅ VERIFIED

### Legislation
- **Bill:** LB 452 (Financial Literacy Act)
- **Signed:** May 26, 2021
- **Effective:** Class of 2024+

### Standards Structure
- **Terminology:** "Statutory Topic" (defined in statute, not DOE framework)
- **Count:** 8 topics
- **Source:** NE Statute 79-3002

### Verified Topic Names (per NE Statute 79-3002)
1. Budget and Financial Record Keeping
2. Banking
3. Taxes
4. Establishing, Building, Maintaining, and Monitoring Credit
5. Debt
6. Savings
7. Risk Management and Insurance
8. Investment Strategies

### Compliance Requirement
LB 452 five-credit financial literacy course graduation requirement (effective 2023-24, Class of 2024+)

### ⚠️ DATA ERROR TO FIX
Nebraska DOES have a state income tax (graduated 2.46% to 6.64%). Remove ALL "no income tax" references in state data files.

## NEW YORK ✅ VERIFIED (MAJOR REWRITE NEEDED)

- **Plan:** NY Inspires Phase 1

### Compliance Requirement
K-12 personal finance education instructional requirement — Grades 5-12 starting 2026-27, Grades K-4 starting 2027-28

### ⚠️ MAJOR CHANGES FROM OLD DATA

- NEW: 5 Topics based on K-12 embedded instruction requirement

## OHIO ✅ VERIFIED

### Legislation
- **Bill:** Senate Bill 1, 134th General Assembly
- **Signed:** October 28, 2021 by Gov. Mike DeWine
- **Effective:** Class of 2026+ (students entering 9th grade on or after July 1, 2022)

### Standards Structure
- **Terminology:** "Topic" (within Learning Standards for Financial Literacy)
- **Count:** 6 topics
- **Standards Adopted:** February 2018
- **Grade Bands:** K-3, 4-6, Middle Grades, High School

### Verified Topic Names (per Ohio DOE Learning Standards)
1. Financial Responsibility and Decision Making
2. Planning and Money Management
3. The Informed Consumer
4. Investing
5. Credit and Debt
6. Risk Management and Insurance

### Compliance Requirement
Senate Bill 1 (signed Oct 28, 2021) — Half-credit standalone course required for Class of 2026+ (students entering 9th grade on or after July 1, 2022). Educator licensure validation required starting 2024-25.

### Educator Requirements (starting 2024-25)
- Teachers must have educator license validation in financial literacy
- Teachers licensed in social studies, family and consumer sciences, or business education are considered qualified without additional validation
