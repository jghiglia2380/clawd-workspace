## Curriculum Alignment - 5 Domains Overview
DOMAIN_1: Domain 1: Fundamentals of Economic Decision-Making (4 standards: SSEF1-4) - Scarcity, rational decision-making, economic systems, and standard of living
DOMAIN_2: Domain 2: Personal Finance (10 standards: SSEPF1-10) - Financial decision-making, budgeting, financial system, interest rates, taxation, credit, insurance, earnings, consumer protection, identity theft
DOMAIN_3: Domain 3: Microeconomics (3 standards: SSEMI1-3) - Household/business interdependence, supply/demand/prices, market structures
DOMAIN_4: Domain 4: Macroeconomics (3 standards: SSEMA1-3) - Measuring economic activity, Federal Reserve, fiscal policy and national debt
DOMAIN_5: Domain 5: International Economics (2 standards: SSEIN1-2) - International trade benefits, exchange rates impact
